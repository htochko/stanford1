This project aims to collect portfolios of masters at different kinds of house repairing services for the sake of to present them to people who need their services to allow find each other easily and fast.

Client can find and estimate level of masters skills at his area check what time master available to apply job and connect him immediately or send him a brief of work he needed to order.

Another great opportunity is that application allows to rate skills and recommend masters they satisfied to other clients.

If you are a master you shouldn't use PC - LEO allows to fill portfolio straightly from phone.

  1. Just install app
  2. Fill photos of projects you already made from phone
  3. Add some Details to understand specific of your work better
  4. Add time table you are available
  5. Suggest other masters in a time you are busy or unavailable
  6. Share your expert knowledge about materials and technologies should be used
  7. Attract new clients and accept more interesting projects!

FAQ

How often can I change range of services that I can supply for my clients?
You can edit your settings as often as you define it necessary - each time only 3 kind of services can be shown as main. Reputation for services you have been declared earlier we be kept in service.
What will happen if I haven't internet connection at a time I filling app with my photos?
All changes done in app on internet absence will be kept and synchronized with server as soon as you get internet connection.
